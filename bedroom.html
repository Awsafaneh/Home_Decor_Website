Bedroom Design Basics
What factors go into deciding the look of your next bedroom design?

1. Color

In choosing colors for a bedroom, make sure you will use neutral colors, warm colors, earth colors and pastel colors. These should be used in your walls, ceilings and even your furniture. Avoid using bright colors for this will not stimulate you to sleep. Make sure also that the colors of your furniture and even fabrics complement each other. ou have to make your room restful and relaxing so that you won’t find it hard to doze off.

2. Choosing the right bed

Your bed is the focal point of the bedroom. Make sure you could choose the right one. If you use a large bed, your bedroom will look small and crowded especially if your room is not too big. If you use a bed which is too small, it will look awkward. You have to choose the right size for your bed for this is one of primary things that could make your room look perfect. Also, choose a good mattress. Something that is soft and comfortable would be perfect for you so that you could have perfect dreams, too.

3. Wall Decor

Some people opt to use wallpapers or have it artistically painted with a unique texture. But others opt to save on wall decor by painting it with plain warm colors and place beautifully framed pictures and paintings. You can actually look for some pictures you like or you might want to consider your kid’s art work. This can help you save a lot.

4. Lighting

Instead of using lights in the middle of the ceiling, use lampshades, up lights and pin lights. Using these type of lightings will give a great mood to your room. It would even be easier for you to rest with that ambience.

5. Space and Storage

Of course, you would always have a cabinet in your room where you will place your clothes and other belongings. It is advisable to have extra cabinet to make sure your things are well kept. Be wise in placing it in order to save space. A bedroom should be well arranged, neat and clutter-free. Having enough storage spaces will help you avoid clutter.
